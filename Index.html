About Us

This innovative solution in the agricultural sector addresses post-harvest waste in the cashew industry. In many African cashew-producing countries, cashew apples are often discarded due to factors such as limited awareness, high perishability, and a lack of processing initiatives. By transforming these overlooked resources into valuable products, this initiative reduces waste, promotes sustainability, and enhances the economic potential of the cashew industry.
