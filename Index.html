Transforming Cashew Fruit into Sustainable Ethanol and Nutritious Food Products

Our innovative process extracts ethanol from cashew apples, providing an eco-friendly alternative to conventional fuel sources. Additionally, we incorporate shrimp and herring powder into our process, creating high-protein, nutrient-rich products that support food security. This solution promotes sustainability, reduces waste, and enhances the livelihoods of agricultural and fishing communities.

Innovator’s Profile

Eric Yankey

A male entrepreneur from Berekum West, Bono Region who has developed an innovative solution in the Agricultural sector to address the issue of post-harvest waste in the cashew industry. Cashew apples are typically left to go to waste in many African cashew-producing countries due to factors like limited knowledge, perishability, and lack of processing initiatives.
